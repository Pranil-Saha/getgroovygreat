Some things that cause global warming

  1. Animal agriculture
  2. Green house gases
  3. Fossil fuels
  4. Littering

how is global warming impacting the world

Increased ocean temperatures are melting glaciers and ice caps all over the world.
Melted ice increases the volume of water in our oceans. Warmer temperatures also result in the expansion of the water's mass,
which causes sea levels to rise, threatening low-lying islands and coastal cities.

What can we do as humans to stop global warming?

  1. Stop littering
  2. Stop cutting down forests
  3. recycle more
